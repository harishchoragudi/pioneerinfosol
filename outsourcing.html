Companies around the world are constantly looking for ways to become more efficient and to reduce costs. The philosophy behind Business Process and Software Development Outsourcing is specific, do what you do best and leave everything else to business process outsourcers. Outsourcing allows a company to free up internal resources, cut development and IT costs by 50% to 75%, and keeps the company in tune with the leading technologies.

Advances in communication technology, such as the virtual networking and task management systems, make offshore software development much more feasible today than it was just ten years ago. The influx of offshore developers has resulted in improved processes for servicing clients and more widely held practices of hiring virtual IT employees.

The key differentiators to using offshore outsourcing and Pioneer Info Solutions services:

  • No employee overhead (desks, computers, office supplies, office space, insurance, taxes, un-employment)
  • No human resource needs (promotions, sick days costs, vacation pay)
  • Highly skilled, world-class developers and engineers
  • Dedicated resources - hired contractor will only work on your projects
  • Broad skill sets - trained in numerous technologies
  • Ability to create 24 hour virtual offices
  • Easy to use project and task management tools for hired resources

Pioneer Info Solutions's outsourcing solutions cover a wide range of business needs, including billing and customer care, insurance administration, healthcare pay and provider management, documentation management, etc. Pioneer Info Solutions's specialists at various facilities across the globe provide 7x24x365 support to every client.
